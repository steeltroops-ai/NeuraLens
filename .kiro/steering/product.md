# NeuraLens Product Overview

NeuraLens is a multi-modal AI-powered neurological assessment platform that transforms healthcare through early detection and comprehensive analysis of neurological conditions.

## Core Product

**Mission**: Democratize access to advanced neurological health screening through AI technology, enabling early detection and improved health outcomes.

**Target Users**: Healthcare professionals, clinicians, and medical researchers conducting neurological assessments.

## Key Features

### Multi-Modal Assessment Suite
- **Speech Pattern Analysis**: Voice biomarker detection for Parkinson's disease screening (95.2% accuracy)
- **Retinal Imaging Assessment**: Non-invasive Alzheimer's disease detection (89.3% accuracy) 
- **Motor Function Evaluation**: Movement pattern analysis with tremor detection (93.7% correlation to clinical UPDRS)
- **Cognitive Testing Suite**: Memory and executive function assessment (91.4% MCI detection accuracy)

### Clinical Excellence
- **NRI Fusion Algorithm**: Multi-modal risk assessment combining all assessment types
- **Real-time Processing**: Sub-200ms response times for clinical workflows
- **HIPAA Compliance**: Healthcare-grade security and privacy protection
- **Clinical Validation**: Evidence-based accuracy with peer-reviewed studies
